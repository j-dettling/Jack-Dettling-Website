Jack Dettling is a pianist and composer who resides in Los Angeles. Their interests include lesser-played Baroque repertoire, experimental works of the 20th-century, player pianos, analog synthesizers, historical and contemporary tuning systems, and the intersection of classical and modern literatures. They hold degrees in piano performance and literature from Mannes College, in contemporary piano performance from Manhattan School of Music, and in piano performacne and composition from CalArts, where they teach currently.
