Jack Dettling is a pianist, composer, and curator who resides in Los Angeles. Their interests include lesser-played Baroque repertoire, experimental works of the 20th-century, player pianos, analog synthesizers, historical and contemporary tuning systems, and the intersection of ancient and modern literatures. As a pianist, they have performed with Piano Spheres, with Monday Evening Concerts, with Jacaranda Music, in living rooms, in galleries, and in concert halls. They have made recordings as a performer and a composer for PopulistRecords. They are the founder and artistic director of beauty salon, a non-profit space for intermedia installation in Los Angeles. They hold degrees in piano performance and literature from Mannes College/The New School, contemporary piano performance from Manhattan School of Music, and piano performance and composition from CalArts, where they teach currently.
